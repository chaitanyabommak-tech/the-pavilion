## Review Strategy

**IMPORTANT:** Only request reviews from genuine visitors/buyers.

### When to ask for reviews:
✅ After a positive site visit
✅ After booking/down payment
✅ After villa handover
✅ From satisfied buyers who moved in

### How to ask:
Send via WhatsApp after site visit:
```
Thank you for visiting The Pavillion!

If you had a positive experience, we'd appreciate if you could share a quick review on Google. It helps other families discover our villa community.

Google Review Link: [Your GBP review link]

Thank you!
Team Bommaku Group
```

### What NOT to do:
❌ Buy fake reviews
❌ Ask friends/family who haven't visited
❌ Offer incentives for 5-star reviews
❌ Write reviews yourself from fake accounts

**Violating Google's review policy can get your profile suspended.**
