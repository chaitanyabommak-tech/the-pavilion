## SALES CALL FLOW (First-Time Enquiry)

### PHASE 1: QUALIFY THE BUYER (5 min)

**Goal:** Understand their needs, budget, timeline, decision-making authority

**Questions to Ask:**
```
1. "What brings you to The Pavillion today?"
   [Listen: Are they actively looking or just browsing?]

2. "Are you currently living in Hyderabad or relocating?"
   [Qualify: Local vs NRI vs other city]

3. "What's prompting you to look for a villa now?"
   [Understand: Trigger event — job change, kids, investment]

4. "Have you seen other villa projects? Which ones?"
   [Competitive intelligence + frame comparison]

5. "What's most important to you in your next home?"
   [Discovery: Priorities — location, space, price, quality]

6. "What's your target timeline for purchase and moving in?"
   [Timeline: Urgency level]

7. "Who else is involved in this decision?"
   [Decision-makers: Spouse, parents, kids]

8. "Have you finalized your budget, or are you exploring options?"
   [Budget: Affordability + financing need]
```

---

### PHASE 2: PRESENT THE PROJECT (15 min)

**Structure:**
1. **Location first** (8 min to Uppal Metro, connectivity map)
2. **Concept** (45 villas, low-density, standalone, design freedom)
3. **Plot & villa types** (150-228 Sq. Yds, 3BHK, G+1+Penthouse)
4. **Amenities** (30,000 SFT recreation, detailed tour)
5. **Differentiators** (no shared walls, customization, limited units)
6. **Trust** (HMDA, banks, developer track record)
7. **Pricing** (₹1.87-3.0 Cr, payment plan, current offers)

**Use Visuals:**
- Master plan (show low density)
- Sample villa elevations (show design freedom)
- Floor plans (show layouts)
- Recreation zone photos (show amenities)
- Location map (show connectivity)

---

### PHASE 3: TRIAL CLOSE (2 min)
