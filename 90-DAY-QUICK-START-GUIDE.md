## RED FLAGS (WHEN TO PIVOT)

**IF by Week 4:**
- GBP views < 100/week → Optimize photos, posts, Q&A
- Google reviews < 5 → Intensify review campaign
- Website traffic < 200/day → Check technical SEO issues
- Cost per lead > ₹800 → Pause ads, optimize campaigns
- Site visit % < 20% → Improve lead quality, better qualification

**IF by Week 8:**
- Keywords still not in top 20 → Check on-page SEO, backlinks
- Paid ad ROI < 1:1 → Pause, re-strategize
- Lead quality poor (< 10% site visits) → Re-target audience
- GBP reviews < 15 → Change review collection approach

**IF by Week 12:**
- Not ranking top 10 for ANY keyword → Hire SEO expert
- Leads < 100/week → Budget increase or channel mix change
- Bookings < 10 in 90 days → Sales process issue (not marketing)
